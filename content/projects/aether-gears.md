An action RPG that explores themes of corporate greed and resource exploitation. This is my first major game development project, which seemed like a perfectly reasonable scope until I actually started building it.

## The Vision

Aether Gears combines fast-paced combat with a narrative about corporate overreach and the consequences of unchecked resource extraction. Think "what if your day job frustrations became a game mechanic?"

## Current Status

Actively in development. Learning every day that game development involves approximately 47 more disciplines than I initially expected.

## What I'm Learning

- Game architecture and state management
- The art of scope management (or the lack thereof)
- Why indie devs drink so much coffee
- That "placeholder art" has a way of becoming permanent
