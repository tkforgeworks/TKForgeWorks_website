A modular organization system designed and 3D-printed to solve real kitchen storage problems. One of the rare projects that's actually finished and in daily use.

## The Problem

Kitchen drawers were a disaster. Cables everywhere, utensils playing musical chairs, and that one junk drawer that had achieved sentient chaos.

## The Solution

Custom-designed modular organizer system that snaps together and can be reconfigured as needs change. Only took three iterations to get right, which by my standards is practically speed-running.

## What I Learned

- Measure twice, print once (or in my case, measure once, print three times)
- The satisfaction of solving a physical problem with engineering
- That "good enough" is sometimes the enemy of "actually done"
